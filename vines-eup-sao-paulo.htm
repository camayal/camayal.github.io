Review of vines genus of Euphorbiaceae s.s. for São Paulo state (Brazil)

Revisão dos gêneros de lianas da família Euphorbiaceae s.s. para o Estado de São Paulo (Brazil)

C.A. Maya-Lastra & I. Cordeiro - 2008

If you need a copy of the final report, that include descriptions and identification keys, please contact with me to facilitate details about it.

After analysis of collections from Brazil, details about the genus Dalechampia and Tragia were compiled and included in one collaborative checklist and a catalog leaded by Instituto de Pesquisas Jardim Botânico do Rio de Janeiro.
